# Authorization Form DLN-0822

This order authorizes Department of Homeland Security (DHS) personnel to engage in enhanced interrogation techniques with Anthony Haven (the "Actor") on August 3rd, 2019. This authorization is subject to the restrictions enumerated below.

1. The Agent may use physical measures to extract information from the Actor.
2. The Agent may use psychological pressure to encourage cooperation.
3. The Agent may restrict the Actor's access to food, water, or sleep as necessary.

Only authorized DHS personnel may conduct the interrogation. This authorization does not apply to any future cases regarding the Actor or to anyone apart from the Actor.
